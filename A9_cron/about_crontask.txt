1. Log Rotation and Cleanup: Automating the rotation and deletion of old log files can help free up disk space and keep the system organized without manual intervention.
2. System Updates: You could schedule automatic updates or security patches to keep your system up-to-date and secure, especially if you tend to forget.

B.
If automated tasks are not managed properly, they could cause problems like accidental data deletion or system overload. For example, a misconfigured backup script might overwrite important data or fail silently, leaving you without a working backup. To avoid this, always test your scripts first, check logs regularly, and set up alerts to notify you if something goes wrong.
